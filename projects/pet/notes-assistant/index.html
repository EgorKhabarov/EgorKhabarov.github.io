Notes Assistant

This is your personal calendar assistant. Here you can easily create events and notes that can be accessed from the calendar. Just use special emoji to add effects or make your search even more convenient! Use all the advantages of the bot to streamline your life and not miss a single important moment! Menu

From the menu you can get to different parts of the bot:

  • Account - Account settings (change password, statistics)
  • Groups - Group settings
  • 7 days - List of notes for the next 7 days
  • Notifications - You can see what the notification will look like on a specific date
  • Bin - Recycle Bin for deleted notes. Notes are stored there for 30 days

Calendar

The calendar has various marks for convenience. For example # indicates the current date. And the small numbers next to the date indicate the number of notes for that day.

Notes actions

You can fully manage your notes

  • - Edit note
  • - Edit note tags
  • - Delete note permanently or move to trash
  • - Beautiful presentation of the note
  • - Change the date of a note
  • - Information about the note
  • - Change history

Event edit

By pressing the button , the text of the note and service information will appear in your input field and you will be able to change the text of the note. Send the modified text and confirm the changes

Settings

Here you can customize the bot for yourself. For example, language, time zone and notifications

Search

You can search notes with various filters. To find something, send the bot a message #<search query>. Put it . instead of <search query> to search all notes

Search filters

By clicking on you can customize your search by adding filters. And by clicking on you will save the search results in CSV format

Help

If you have any questions, enter the /help command
